# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales & Customer Insight Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes E-commerce sales data to uncover key business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance
- Identify high-value and repeat customers
- Understand customer segment contribution
- Support business strategy

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- SQL (Data querying & analysis)
- Excel (Data cleaning)
- Power BI (Dashboard & visualization)

## 📊 Key Insights
- Repeat customers drive majority of revenue
- Sales increase towards year-end months
- Consumer segment contributes highest sales
- Revenue concentrated among top customers

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus on customer retention strategies
- Introduce loyalty programs
- Increase stock during peak months
- Target high-value customers

## 🧾 SQL Queries Included
- Total Sales Calculation
- Top Products Analysis
- Region-wise Sales
- Monthly Sales Trends
- Repeat Customer Analysis
- Profit by Category
